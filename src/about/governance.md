# Governance

JuliaGeo is governed by a Steering Committee responsible for technical oversight, project approval, and policy decisions. The full organizational charter is available in the [governance repository](https://github.com/JuliaGeo/governance).

If you have any disputes that you would like mediated, please send an email to [steering@juliageo.org](mailto:steering@juliageo.org).
